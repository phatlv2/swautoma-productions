# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 27, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the wallpaper applications developed by **Phat La** and published under the SwAutoma brand, including but not limited to:

- [S8 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinitys8)
- [N8 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinityn8)
- [S9 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinity)
- [S24 Ultra Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.s24uinfinity)

These applications are distributed worldwide.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The applications do not require user registration and do not collect personal information such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Home address  
- Payment information  

However, the applications use **Firebase Crashlytics**, a service provided by Google, to collect limited technical information in the event of application crashes.

Crashlytics may automatically collect:

- Device model  
- Operating system version  
- App version  
- Crash logs  
- Anonymous device identifiers  

This information is used solely to improve application stability and performance. It does not personally identify users.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 2. Internet Usage

The applications require an internet connection to download wallpaper images from remote servers.

No user accounts are required, and no personal data is transmitted to the developer’s own servers.

---

## 3. Local Storage

Downloaded wallpapers may be stored locally on the user’s device for the purpose of setting and displaying wallpapers.

The app does not access personal files, contacts, photos, messages, or other private user data.

---

## 4. Advertising

These applications do not display advertisements and do not use advertising SDKs.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

These applications are suitable for general audiences, including children under the age of 13.

The applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The only automatically collected information is anonymous technical crash data via Firebase Crashlytics, which does not identify individual users.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Crash-related technical data is processed by Google Firebase under their privacy policy.

---

## 7. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect application integrity. Since no personal user accounts or sensitive personal data are stored, the risk of personal data exposure is minimized.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

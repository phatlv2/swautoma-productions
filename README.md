# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the wallpaper applications developed by **Phát La** and published under the SwAutoma brand, including:

- [S8 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinitys8)
- [N8 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinityn8)
- [S9 Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinity)
- [S24 Ultra Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.s24uinfinity)
- [Galaxy Infinity Wallpaper](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.swautoma.wallpaper.infinityaio)

These applications are distributed worldwide.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The applications do not require user registration and do not collect personal information such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Home address  

**Payment Information:**
- Only **Galaxy Infinity Wallpaper** offers in-app purchases.
- All payments are processed securely through Google Play.
- The developer does not collect, store, or have access to users' payment details such as credit card numbers or billing information.

However, the applications may use third-party services (listed below) that automatically collect limited technical and device-related information for crash reporting and advertising.

---

## 2. Advertising (AdMob)

**Galaxy Infinity Wallpaper** (and any other app in this list that contains ads) uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect and process information such as:

- Advertising ID (or other device identifiers)  
- Ad interaction data (e.g., ad impressions, clicks)  
- Approximate device information (model, OS version)  

This information is used to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and improve ad performance. The developer does not directly receive personally identifying information from AdMob.

---

## 3. Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The applications use **Firebase Crashlytics**, a service provided by Google, to collect limited technical information in the event of application crashes.

Crashlytics may automatically collect:

- Device model  
- Operating system version  
- App version  
- Crash logs  
- Anonymous device identifiers  

This information is used solely to improve application stability and performance.

---

## 4. Internet Usage

The applications may require an internet connection to download wallpaper images from remote servers.

No user accounts are required, and no personal data is transmitted to the developer’s own servers for account or profile purposes.

---

## 5. Local Storage

Downloaded wallpapers may be stored locally on the user’s device for the purpose of setting and displaying wallpapers.

The apps do not access personal contacts, SMS, call logs, calendars, microphones, or cameras.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Some technical and device-related data may be processed by Google services used in the apps:

- Google AdMob (for ads, if enabled in an app)
- Firebase Crashlytics (for crash reporting)
- Google Play Billing (only in Galaxy Infinity Wallpaper, for processing in-app purchases)

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

These applications are intended for a general audience.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If ads are shown in an app (e.g., Galaxy Infinity Wallpaper), they are provided through Google AdMob and may collect device identifiers for advertising and measurement purposes.

Parents or guardians can contact the developer if they have questions or concerns.

---

## 8. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect application integrity. Since no user accounts or sensitive personal data are stored by the developer, the risk of personal data exposure is minimized.
